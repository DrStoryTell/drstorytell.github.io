DrStoryTell Secretariat

DrStoryTell Secretariat provides services to help support the execution of your festival because there are many moving parts that you may require assistance and attention with.

Services offered include:
(1) co-ordination of science focused outreach activities.
(2) hosting and chairing of pre-festival planning meetings.
(3) organising of registration area for attendees.
(4) sourcing and co-ordinating with media, NGO/CBO affiliates and vendors.
(5) assisting with preparation and distribution of feedback/evaluation forms.
(6) preparation and distribution of certificates and tokens post-festival.
