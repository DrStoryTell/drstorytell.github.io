One documentary filmaker, Brian Lindstorm, said that,"If I weren't making documentary films, I suppose I'd be teaching." DrStoryTell Films provides us with the opportunity to do both.
Dr. Albertha Joseph-Alexander (DrStoryTell) desires to tell the stories behind the plants and crops we love. When the story includes research, discovery and history then DrStoryTell Films is happy to tell you about the plot.

Presently DrStoryTell is producing her first film entitled "We DreamPeas". It is a film about the people living in Trinidad and Tobago who cherish their Pigeonpeas culture and cuisine.
